## Testing Phases

1. **Internal Testing** (Current step)
   - Add up to 100 testers by email
   - Test core functionality
   - Fix critical bugs

2. **Closed Testing** 
   - Larger group of testers
   - Gather feedback
   - Performance testing

3. **Open Testing**
   - Public beta
   - Stress testing
   - Final adjustments

4. **Production Release**
   - Full public launch
