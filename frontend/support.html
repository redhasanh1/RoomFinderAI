Getting Started

Click the "Sign Up" button in the top navigation, fill in your details including email, password, and phone number. You'll receive a verification email to complete your registration.

Go to the Listings page and use our advanced filters to search by location, price range, number of bedrooms, and amenities. You can also save your searches for quick access later.

Our AI Negotiator is a smart assistant that helps you find properties matching your specific needs and can assist with landlord communications, price negotiations, and answering questions about properties.

Account & Billing

Basic features including property search and viewing listings are free. Premium features like AI Negotiator, advanced filters, and priority support require a subscription starting at $9.99/month.

Go to your Dashboard and click on "Profile Settings". You can update your personal information, preferences, and upload a profile picture from there.

You can cancel your subscription anytime from your Dashboard under "Billing Settings". Your access to premium features will continue until the end of your current billing period.

Property Management

Each property listing has a "Contact Landlord" button. Click it to send a message directly through our platform. You can also let our AI Negotiator handle initial communications for you.

Technical Support

Try resetting your password using the "Forgot Password" link on the login page. If you still can't access your account, contact support@roomfinderai.com with your email address.

Try clearing your browser cache and cookies, or use a different browser. Ensure you have a stable internet connection. If issues persist, our technical team can help.

Email support@roomfinderai.com with details about the issue, including what you were doing when it occurred, any error messages, and your browser/device information.
