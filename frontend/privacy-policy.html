Privacy Policy for RoomFinder

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Name and email address (for account creation)
  • Profile information you choose to provide
  • Messages and communications through our chat features
  • Property listings and photos you upload

Usage Information:

  • App usage analytics and performance data
  • Device information (device type, OS version)
  • Location data (only when you choose to share it for property searches)
  • Search queries and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Property Matching: To help you find suitable rental properties
  • AI Negotiation: To assist with rent negotiations using our AI features
  • Communication: To enable messaging between users and property owners
  • Account Management: To maintain and secure your account
  • App Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and improve our services

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in these situations:

  • With Other Users: Property listings and messages you choose to share
  • Service Providers: Third-party services that help us operate the app (with strict privacy agreements)
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect user safety
  • Business Transfer: In case of merger or acquisition (users will be notified)

4. AI and Machine Learning

Our app uses AI technology to:

  • Provide property recommendations
  • Assist with rent negotiations
  • Improve search results

AI processing is done securely and your data is used only to provide these services to you.

5. Data Security

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard SSL/TLS
  • User passwords are hashed and securely stored
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal data by authorized personnel only

6. Your Rights and Controls

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Update: Modify your account information anytime
  • Delete: Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt-out: Disable certain features or data collection
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a machine-readable format

7. Children's Privacy

RoomFinder is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

8. Location Services

Location data is collected only when you explicitly enable location services. This helps us:

  • Show properties near your current location
  • Provide relevant search results
  • Improve map functionality

You can disable location services in your device settings at any time.

9. Third-Party Services

Our app may integrate with:

  • Google Maps: For location and mapping features
  • Analytics Services: To understand app usage and performance
  • Cloud Storage: For secure data backup and synchronization

These services have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review.

10. International Users

For users outside the United States:

  • We comply with applicable international privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  • Data may be transferred and stored in the United States
  • Adequate data protection measures are maintained during transfers

11. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your preferences and login status
  • Analyze app performance and usage
  • Provide personalized content and recommendations

12. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Messages: Stored for up to 2 years for quality and safety purposes
  • Usage Analytics: Anonymized data may be retained indefinitely for service improvement
  • Property Listings: Deleted when you remove them or close your account

13. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically. Changes will be:

  • Posted in the app and on our website
  • Notified via email for significant changes
  • Effective 30 days after posting (unless immediate changes are required by law)

14. Your Choices

You can control your privacy by:

  • Adjusting privacy settings in the app
  • Limiting what information you share in your profile
  • Choosing what data to sync across devices
  • Opting out of certain analytics and marketing communications

15. Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or your personal data:

  • Email: privacy@roomfinderai.com
  • Website: https://roomfinderai.com/privacy
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 48 hours

For EU residents: You have the right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority if you believe our processing of your personal data violates applicable law.

This privacy policy is effective as of January 2025 and applies to all users of the RoomFinder mobile application.
